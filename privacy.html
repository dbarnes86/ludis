Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Who we are

This Privacy Policy applies to Ludis Management Ltd, a company registered in England and Wales. Our website is lud.is. When we refer to "we", "us" or "our" in this policy, we mean Ludis Management Ltd. When we refer to "you", we mean any person who visits our website or contacts us.

What information we collect

We collect information you provide directly to us. This typically happens when you contact us by email. The information you provide may include your name, email address, company name, and any other details you choose to share in your message.

We do not use tracking cookies, analytics tools, or any third-party scripts that collect data about your visit to this website. This site does not use cookies.

How we use your information

We use the information you provide solely to respond to your enquiry and, where a business relationship follows, to carry out the services we have agreed with you. We do not sell, share, or rent your personal data to any third party. We do not use your data for marketing purposes without your explicit consent.

Legal basis for processing

Where you are based in the UK or European Economic Area, we process your personal data on the basis of legitimate interests (responding to enquiries and delivering services) and, where applicable, contract performance. Where we require your consent, we will ask for it explicitly.

How long we keep your information

We retain correspondence and engagement records for as long as is necessary to fulfil the purpose for which they were collected, or as required by applicable law. For general enquiries that do not result in an engagement, we will not retain your information beyond the period reasonably necessary to respond.

Your rights

If you are based in the UK or EEA, you have the right to access the personal data we hold about you, to request correction or deletion of that data, to object to or restrict our processing, and to request a copy of your data in a portable format. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at dan@lud.is.

If you are based in California, you may have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information we collect and the right to request deletion. We do not sell personal information. To exercise your rights, contact us at the address above.

Data security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from unauthorised access, use, or disclosure. Communications sent to us by email are subject to standard internet security practices. We recommend you exercise care when transmitting sensitive information by email.

Third-party services

This website is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect limited technical data such as IP addresses as part of their standard hosting infrastructure. This is governed by GitHub's own privacy policy, which can be found at github.com/site/privacy.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact

If you have any questions about this policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at dan@lud.is.
